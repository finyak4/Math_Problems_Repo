## Function Analysis - Solutions

### Ex 1. Analyze the function

**1)** $y = x^2 \ln x$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = x(2\ln x + 1)$
- **Extrema:** $y'=0$ at $x = e^{-1/2}$. Minimum.

**2)** $y = (\ln x)^3 - 3 \ln x$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{3}{x}((\ln x)^2 - 1)$
- **Extrema:** $x = e$ (Min), $x = 1/e$ (Max).

**3)** $y = \frac{1}{\ln x}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x \in (0,1) \cup (1, \infty)$
- **Derivative:** $y' = -x^{-1}(\ln x)^{-2}$. No extrema.

**4)** $y = \ln x + \frac{1}{\ln x}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x \in (0,1) \cup (1, \infty)$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{(\ln x)^2-1}{x(\ln x)^2}$
- **Extrema:** $x=e, x=1/e$.

**5)** $y = \frac{1}{2} x^2 \ln \frac{1}{2} x$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = x(\ln \frac{x}{2} + \frac{1}{2})$
- **Extrema:** $x = 2e^{-1/2}$.

**6)** $y = x - 2 \ln x$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = 1 - 2/x$
- **Extrema:** $x=2$ (Min).

**7)** $y = \frac{1+\ln x}{x}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = -\frac{\ln x}{x^2}$
- **Extrema:** $x=1$ (Max).

**8)** $y = \ln(x^2-1) + \frac{1}{x^2-1}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $|x| > 1$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{2x(x^2-2)}{(x^2-1)^2}$
- **Extrema:** $x = \pm\sqrt{2}$.

**9)** $y = \ln \sqrt{x^2+1}-1$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $\mathbb{R}$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{x}{x^2+1}$
- **Extrema:** $x=0$ (Min).

**10)** $y = \frac{\ln x}{\sqrt{x}}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x > 0$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{2-\ln x}{2x\sqrt{x}}$
- **Extrema:** $x=e^2$ (Max).

**11)** $y = \ln \left(e + \frac{1}{x}\right)$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $x \in (-\infty, -1/e) \cup (0, \infty)$
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{-1}{x(ex+1)}$. No extrema.

**12)** $y = \ln(1+e^{-x})$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{-e^{-x}}{1+e^{-x}}$. Always negative. No extrema.

**13)** $y = \ln \sin x$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = \operatorname{ctg} x$.
- **Extrema:** Maxima at $\pi/2 + 2k\pi$.

**14)** $y = \ln \text{tg}(\frac{\pi}{4}-\frac{1}{2}x)$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = -1/\cos x$. No extrema.

**15)** $y = \frac{e^{-x}}{x^2-1}$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{-e^{-x}(x^2+2x-1)}{(x^2-1)^2}$.
- **Extrema:** $x = -1 \pm \sqrt{2}$.

**16)** $y = \frac{x}{e^{x}-1}$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{e^x(1-x)-1}{(e^x-1)^2}$.
- **Extrema:** $x=0$ by limit (removable disc), derivative is $-1/2$. Solution to $e^x(1-x)=1$ is $x=0$.

**17)** $y = e^{\frac{1}{x^2-1}}$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = \frac{-2xy}{(x^2-1)^2}$.
- **Extrema:** $x=0$.

**18)** $y = e^{\frac{1}{x}}(x+1)$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = e^{1/x} \frac{x^2-x-1}{x^2}$.
- **Extrema:** $x = \frac{1 \pm \sqrt{5}}{2}$.

**20)** $y = e^{-x^2}$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** $y' = -2x y$.
- **Extrema:** Max at $x=0$.

**21)** $y = e^{-x^2+8x-14}$
**Solution:**
- **Extrema:** Max at $x=4$.

**22)** $y = xe^{-\frac{1}{2}x^2}$
**Solution:**
- **Extrema:** $x = \pm 1$.

**23)** $y = x^2 e^{1/x}$
**Solution:**
- **Extrema:** Min at $x=1/2$.

**24)** $y = x^3 e^{-4x}$
**Solution:**
- **Extrema:** Max at $x=3/4$.

**29)** $y = e^{\arcsin \sqrt{x}}$
**Solution:**
- **Domain:** $0 \le x \le 1$.
- **Derivative:** Positive in domain. No local extrema inside.

**30)** $y = \text{arctg}(\ln x)$
**Solution:**
- **Derivative:** Positive. No extrema.
