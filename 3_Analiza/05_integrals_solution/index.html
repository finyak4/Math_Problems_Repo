Integrals - Solutions

Ex 1. Calculate the integrals

1) \(\int \frac{x\sqrt{x} - x\sqrt[4]{x}}{\sqrt[3]{x}} dx\) Solution: $$ \int (x^{7/6} - x^{11/12}) dx = \frac{6}{13}x^{13/6} - \frac{12}{23}x^{23/12} $$

2) \(\int (3+2\sqrt[4]{x})^3 dx\) Solution: $$ 27x + \frac{216}{5}x^{5/4} + 24x^{3/2} + \frac{32}{7}x^{7/4} $$

3) \(\int \frac{\sqrt{x}-2\sqrt[3]{x^2}+4\sqrt[6]{x^3}}{6\sqrt[3]{x}} dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{7}x^{7/6} - \frac{1}{2}x^{4/3} + \frac{2}{7}x^{7/6} = \frac{3}{7}x^{7/6} - \frac{1}{2}x^{4/3} $$

4) \(\int \frac{3+5\sqrt[3]{x^2}}{\sqrt[3]{x^2}} dx\) Solution: $$ \int (3x^{-2/3} + 5) dx = 9x^{1/3} + 5x $$

5) \(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{3x+1}}\) Solution: $$ \frac{2}{3}\sqrt{3x+1} $$

13) \(\int e^{1/x} \frac{dx}{x^2}\) Solution: $$ -e^{1/x} $$

14) \(\int x e^{-x^2} dx\) Solution: $$ -\frac{1}{2}e^{-x^2} $$

15) \(\int \frac{dx}{2\cos^2 3x}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{6}\operatorname{tg} 3x $$

16) \(\int x \sin(2x^2+1) dx\) Solution: $$ -\frac{1}{4}\cos(2x^2+1) $$

17) \(\int \sin^3 x \cos x dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{4}\sin^4 x $$

18) \(\int \frac{\cos x}{\sqrt{1+\sin x}} dx\) Solution: $$ 2\sqrt{1+\sin x} $$

20) \(\int \cos x \cdot e^{\sin x} dx\) Solution: $$ e^{\sin x} $$

21) \(\int \frac{x^3 dx}{\cos^2 x^4}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{4}\operatorname{tg}(x^4) $$

22) \(\int \frac{\operatorname{tg} x}{\cos^2 x} dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{2}\operatorname{tg}^2 x $$

23) \(\int \frac{x^2 dx}{\cos^2(x^3+1)}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{3}\operatorname{tg}(x^3+1) $$

24) \(\int \frac{(\ln x)^2}{x} dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{3}(\ln x)^3 $$

25) \(\int \frac{dx}{e^x+e^{-x}} = \int \frac{e^x}{e^{2x}+1} dx\) Solution: $$ \operatorname{arctg}(e^x) $$

26) \(\int \frac{e^x dx}{2e^x+1}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{2}\ln(2e^x+1) $$

27) \(\int x \ln(1+x^2) dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{2}((x^2+1)\ln(1+x^2) - x^2) $$

38) \(\int x^2 e^x dx\) Solution: $$ e^x(x^2-2x+2) $$

41) \(\int x \cos x dx\) Solution: $$ x\sin x + \cos x $$

44) \(\int e^x \cos x dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{e^x}{2}(\sin x + \cos x) $$

46) \(\int e^x \cos^2 x dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{e^x}{2} + \frac{e^x}{10}(\sin 2x + 2\cos 2x) $$

55) \(\int \frac{2x-13}{(x-5)^2} dx\) Solution: $$ 2\ln|x-5| + \frac{3}{x-5} $$

57) \(\int \frac{dx}{2x^2-2x+5}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{3} \operatorname{arctg} \frac{2x-1}{3} $$

71) \(\int \frac{4x-5}{x^2-6x+10} dx\) Solution: $$ 2\ln(x^2-6x+10) + 7\operatorname{arctg}(x-3) $$

84) \(\int \sqrt{2x+1} dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{3}(2x+1)^{3/2} $$

88) \(\int x \sqrt[3]{x-4} dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{3}{7}(x-4)^{7/3} + 3(x-4)^{4/3} $$

106) \(\int \cos 5x \cos 7x dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{\sin 12x}{24} + \frac{\sin 2x}{4} $$

115) \(\int \sin^3 x dx\) Solution: $$ -\cos x + \frac{1}{3}\cos^3 x $$

136) \(\int \frac{dx}{e^{2x}-1}\) Solution: $$ \frac{1}{2}\ln|e^{2x}-1| - x \quad \text{or} \quad \frac{1}{2}\ln|\tanh x|? \quad \text{Use } u=e^x: \frac{1}{2}\ln|\frac{e^x-1}{e^x+1}| $$

151) \(\int \frac{dx}{x \ln x}\) Solution: $$ \ln|\ln x| $$

152) \(\int \ln(x^2+1) dx\) Solution: $$ x \ln(x^2+1) - 2x + 2\operatorname{arctg} x $$

160) \(\int x a^x dx\) Solution: $$ \frac{a^x}{\ln a}(x - \frac{1}{\ln a}) $$
